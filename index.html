About New Graceland Medical Centre

The heart, mission, and people behind our promise.

Our Story

New Graceland Medical Centre was born from a simple yet powerful belief: healthcare is a fundamental right, not a privilege reserved for a few. Witnessing countless individuals struggle to access proper medical care due to financial constraints, our founder was inspired by a surge of calling to create a haven of health and hope. We started as a small clinic with a big heart, dedicated to serving those who felt left behind. Today, we continue that legacy, reinforcing our commitment to making quality healthcare accessible to everyone in our community.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission:

To ensure people enjoy health at an affordable cost, delivered with excellence, compassion, and unwavering integrity.

Vision:

To be the most trusted healthcare provider in the region, a place that restores hope, promotes total wellness, and builds a healthier future for all.
